## Frequently Asked Questions
Everything you need to know about our banking services.
How long does account opening take?
Most accounts are approved within minutes. Identity verification may take up to 24 hours for additional review.
Is there a minimum deposit required?
No! You can open an account with $0 and start saving immediately. No minimum balance requirements.
Are my deposits FDIC insured?
Yes, all deposits are FDIC insured up to $250,000 through our partner banks.
Can I access my account internationally?
Yes! Use your card in 190+ countries and manage 50+ currencies through our mobile app.
What fees should I expect?
We believe in transparent banking. No monthly fees, no overdraft fees, and no minimum balance fees.
How does the AI financial advisor work?
Our AI analyzes your spending patterns and provides personalized insights, savings recommendations, and investment opportunities tailored to your goals.
Can I open a business account?
Absolutely! We offer comprehensive business banking solutions including checking, savings, merchant services, and business loans with dedicated support.
